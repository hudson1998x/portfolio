===========================================
  YOUR FILES GO HERE
===========================================

This directory is yours. Anything you place
here will never be touched by a Codefolio
update.

-------------------------------------------
  WHY THE SEPARATION?
-------------------------------------------

Codefolio separates userland from framework
code for three reasons:

  1. SAFE UPDATES
     Pulling a new version of Codefolio will
     never overwrite, merge, or delete
     anything in this directory.

  2. CLEAR OWNERSHIP
     Anything in here is yours. Anything
     outside is ours. No ambiguity.

  3. PORTABILITY
     If you ever migrate away from Codefolio,
     your content, modules, and customisations
     are already cleanly isolated — just take
     this directory with you.

-------------------------------------------
  WHAT BELONGS HERE?
-------------------------------------------

  - Custom controllers & admin pages
  - Your content entities
  - Site-specific services
  - Component overrides
  - Anything you've built on top of Codefolio

-------------------------------------------
  WHAT DOESN'T BELONG HERE?
-------------------------------------------

  - Framework internals  (leave those alone)
  - node_modules         (managed by npm)
  - build output         (managed by esbuild)
