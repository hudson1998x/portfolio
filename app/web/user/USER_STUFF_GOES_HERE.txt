===========================================
  YOUR FRONTEND FILES GO HERE
===========================================

This directory is yours. Anything you place
here will never be touched by a Codefolio
update.

-------------------------------------------
  WHY THE SEPARATION?
-------------------------------------------

Codefolio separates your frontend from the
framework renderer for three reasons:

  1. SAFE UPDATES
     Pulling a new version of Codefolio will
     never overwrite, merge, or delete
     your components, styles, or assets.

  2. CLEAR OWNERSHIP
     Your components are yours. The renderer
     is ours. No ambiguity, no conflicts.

  3. PORTABILITY
     Your TSX components and stylesheets are
     plain React and CSS — no proprietary
     syntax to unpick if you ever move on.

-------------------------------------------
  WHAT BELONGS HERE?
-------------------------------------------

  - Your components (TSX)
  - Your stylesheets (CSS / SCSS)
  - Static assets (fonts, images, icons)
  - Component defaults & prop definitions
  - Anything visual you've built for your site

-------------------------------------------
  WHAT DOESN'T BELONG HERE?
-------------------------------------------

  - The renderer        (that's ours)
  - The router          (that's ours)
  - build output        (managed by esbuild)
  - node_modules        (managed by npm)

-------------------------------------------
  HOW COMPONENTS WORK
-------------------------------------------

Every component you register here becomes
available in the page editor palette.

Export a defaults object alongside your
render function and Codefolio handles the
rest — prop panels, scaffolding, and config
are all derived from your defaults
automatically.

  defaults  →  prop panel fields
  defaults  →  new page scaffolding
  defaults  →  useModuleConfig fallback

No extra configuration needed.
